Why Should You Work With Me?

Because I've been there.

Tools breaking, deadlines closing in, and 'survival-mode fixes' just about keeping things afloat.

We made it work — but after years of experience, I stepped back and asked: what if we stopped firefighting and started building smarter?

That's the space I work in now.

I help teams get more from the tools, systems, and processes they already have — by leaning into integrations and surfacing new or overlooked features that can drive real efficiency and impact.

And yes, I use AI — confidently.

Because the right tech doesn't replace us. It helps us stay objective, unlock ideas, remove bottlenecks, and free up time for the work that really matters.

I'm not a one-man band, but a part of a modern, lean, and smart setup.

Now, I use my experience and platform knowledge to help teams focus on what truly moves the needle.
